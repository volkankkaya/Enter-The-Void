ABOUT US



Enter the Void is a program dedicated to the exploration of the dark and deep nuances of
electronic music genres. Takes place on Aaja Music Radio in Deptford (LDN) every other thursday
of every month at 11 PM (UK time).

DJ Void (he/him) is an italian-born and raised electronic producer who has brought his unique
sonic vision from his years in the UK to the heart of Copenhagen.

Known for his versatility, DJ Void effortlessly crosses genres, mixing British bass, old-school
progressive Goa and deep, hypnotically fast techno and trance into his dynamic performances.

His ability to curate diverse but coherent sets has made him a prominent figure in the Danish
electronic music scene.

Over the past few years has DJ Void strengthened his presence in Copenhagen's underground
circuit, taking over the decks at iconic venues such as Den Anden Side, Module, Hangaren and
Baggen / Jolene.
